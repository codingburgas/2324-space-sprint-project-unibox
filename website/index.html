WHAT IS

UniBox

Our solar system sandbox is an interactive simulation
allowing users to adjust speed, add black holes, planets,
and change directions, providing an educational
experience in celestial mechanics and space exploration.

What Can You Do With UniBox?

By using change direction button the planets will turn back their direction from left to right to right to left.

CHANGE SPEED
